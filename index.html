💬 核心句型 / Key Sentence Patterns
🏖️ Unit 1 — 暑假故事 Summer Vacation
❓ How was your summer vacation?
你的暑假怎么样？
→ It was wonderful! / It was meaningful!
❓ Where did you go?
你去哪里了？
→ I went to Beijing. / I went to the beach.
❓ How did you get there?
你是怎么去那里的？
→ By train. / By bus. / On foot. / By plane.
❓ What did you do there?
你在那里做了什么？
→ I visited the Great Wall. / I swam in the sea.
🚑 Unit 2 — 安全与健康 Safety & Health
✅ We should wear a helmet when we ride a bike.
骑自行车时我们应该戴头盔。
We should / We shouldn't + 动词原形
💡 It's better to call 119 in a fire.
火灾时最好拨打119。
It's better to + 动词原形
😷 I have a stomachache / toothache / headache.
我胃痛 / 牙痛 / 头痛。
I have a + 疾病名称
🤝 How can I help you?
我能帮你什么？
→ You should rest and drink more water.
🚂 Unit 3 — 科技与生活 Technology & Life
🌐 Technology is changing our communication / learning.
科技正在改变我们的通信方式 / 学习方式。
Technology is changing + 名词
⏳ In the past, people wrote letters. Now, people send emails.
过去，人们写信。现在，人们发电子邮件。
In the past... Now...
🚄 The Qinghai-Xizang Railway went into service in 2006.
青藏铁路于2006年建成通车。
went into service = 开始运营
🌟 Unit 5 — 名人榜样 Role Models
⭐ He / She is famous for his/her achievements.
他/她以成就而闻名。
be famous for + 名词/动名词
💫 He / She is famous because he/she worked hard.
他/她因为努力工作而出名。
be famous because + 句子
🙌 He / She is my role model.
他/她是我的榜样。
用你心中的榜样代替 He/She！
🌈 I want to be a scientist like him / her.
我想像他/她一样成为一名科学家。
I want to be + 职业 + like him/her

🎭 对话示例 Sample Dialogue

Tom: How was your summer vacation, Lily?

Lily: It was wonderful! I went to Beijing with my family.

Tom: Really? How did you get there?

Lily: By train. It was very fast!

Tom: What did you do there?

Lily: I visited the Great Wall and the Palace Museum. It was meaningful.

Tom: Wow, that sounds amazing!

💡 学习小贴士 / Study Tips

🗣️ 口语练习 Speaking Practice

  • 每天大声朗读10分钟 Read aloud 10 min daily
  • 跟着音频模仿语音语调 Imitate the audio pronunciation
  • 对着镜子练习口语 Practice speaking in front of a mirror
  • 用中文想一想，再翻译成英文 Think in Chinese first, then translate

📝 单词记忆 Vocabulary Tips

  • 用单词造句，不只背单词 Make sentences, don't just memorize
  • 把单词写在便利贴上贴到墙上 Write words on sticky notes
  • 每天复习昨天的单词 Review yesterday's words every day
  • 用单词卡片（闪卡）反复练习 Use flashcards to practice repeatedly

✍️ 写作技巧 Writing Tips

  • 先列提纲再动笔 Outline before you write
  • 用上课本里的句型 Use sentence patterns from the textbook
  • 写完后大声读一遍检查 Read aloud after writing to check
  • 注意大写和标点符号 Check capitalization and punctuation
